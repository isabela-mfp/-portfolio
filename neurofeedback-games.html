Neurofeedback Games
One of the types of feedback in BT2 Software is game feedback. It joins game mechanics with neurofeedback training objectives to reach the goals. All ten games in the project were made using the open source game engine Godot. Its scripts are written in a language derived from Python, called GDScript. Our game design and development team was made up of two people. We presented ideas for each game according to the requested specifications and analyzed them to choose the one that best met the requirements. We also did the concept arts and most of the assets. The challenge of this project was to create different mechanics for each game that meet the requested neurofeedback training requirements.
The part I liked the most was work with the game structure and how data and brain signals input are processed and used to generate an intuitive feedback.
